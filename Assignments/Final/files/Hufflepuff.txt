The house of the kind and loyal, Hufflepuff is the house of the badgers (and home to Cedric Diggory, Teddy Lupin, and Nymphadora Tonks). The character traits of this house are: hard-working, friendly, loyal, honest, and impartial. Generally, Hufflepuffs are regarded as dedicated and nice.

Decorated in yellow and black, Hufflepuff Basement is guarded by repelling devices. Near the kitchen, there is a pile of barrels stacked up. The passage to the common room will only open if the barrel two from the bottom, middle of the second row is tapped in the rhythm of 'Helga Hufflepuff'. Additionally, Hufflepuffs are supported by their house ghost: Fat Friar. During his lifetime, he was sorted into Hufflepuff. He is said to be very jolly and forgiving. 

During the time of Harry Potter's education, the head of Hufflepuff was Pomona Sprout. Additionally, the world renowned Magizoologist Newton Scamander was a Hufflepuff. More about Scamander can be found in 'Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them'.

The house of Hufflepuff was founded by Helga Hufflepuff during medieval times. While alive, she selected students based on their nothing. Hufflepuff accepts all students, however they are typically described as loyal and hard-working. Like the other founders, she also possessed a magical artifact. Her's was a small golden cup that Tom Riddle (Voldemort) would later taint as a horcrux to ensure his immortality.
