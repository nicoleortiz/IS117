Albus Percival Wulfric Brian Dumbledore

DOB: Summer 1881
DOD: June 30, 1997
Blood-Status: Half-Blood
Wand: 15" Thestral tail hair (Elder Wand)
House: Gryffindor
Patronus: Phoenix
Series Role: Dumbledore was the Professor of Transfiguration during Tom Riddle's time at Hogwarts. Additionally, he was the one to inform young Riddle that he was a wizard. He watched helplessly as Tom Riddle grew to become a Dark Lord (Dumbledore defeated the previous dark lord Grindelwald). Later, he became Headmaster of Hogwarts during the time of Harry Potter and his parents. Dumbledore led the secret group The Order of the Phoenix to bring down Voldemort. He was also a grandfatherly figure for Harry Potter.



Severus Tobias Snape

DOB: January 9, 1960
DOD: May 2, 1998
Blood-Status: Half-Blood
Wand: unknown
House: Slytherin
Patronus: Doe
Series Role: During his own time attending Hogwarts, Snape fell in love with Lily Potter nee Evans. They were best friends prior to their magical schooling and grew closer during their magical education. However, they had a falling out with pushed Lily to James Potter (who she ended up marrying). When Lily had a child and the prophecy was made about Harry, Snape begged Dumbledore to protect them. When she died, he vowed to protect Harry from harm. During Harry's time at Hogwarts, Snape used the facade of hatred for the boy to protect his role as a spy. This was easier due to the fact that Harry looked exactly like his father (whom Snape despised). Snape played the role of a trusted friend to Dumbledore and loyal follower of Voldemort. In the end, Snape was really fighting to protect Harry because of his love for Lily.



Sirius Orion Black

DOB: November 3, 1959
DOD: June 18, 1996
Blood-Status: Pure Blood
Wand: unknown
House: Gryffindor (first Black not in Slytherin)
Patronus: unknown
Series Role: During his time at Hogwarts, Sirius was part of a group called the Marauders. Essentially this was a group of James Potter and his best friends. When James and Lily Potter were murdered, Sirius went after Peter Pettigrew who he knew to be repsonsible for their deaths. However, in his rage the Aurors (wizard police) assumed that he was involved in the murder and put in Azkaban (wizard prison). Until he broke out to see Harry in his third year of schooling. Sirius began to be Harry's closest family member, almost falling into a fatherly role for the boy. In Harry's fifth year, Voldemort broke into the Ministry of Magic and a battle between Voldemort and his allies broke out with Harry and his friends/family. During this battle, Sirius was hit by a curse that pushed him into the veil (the doorway between the living and the dead. His death destroyed Harry.



Lord Voldemort (Tom Marvolo Riddle)

DOB: December 31, 1926
DOD: May 2, 1998
Blood-Status: Half-Blood
Wand: 13.5" Yew, Phoenix feather core
House: Slytherin
Patronus: unable to produce
Series Role: During his time at Hogwarts, Tom was a very intelligent student. He was said to have a way with people and passion for learning. He grew up in an orphanage and longed to escape the muggle world. Over time, he grew obsessed with leaving the muggle world (as every summer he was returned to the orphanage) and hated the muggles who hated him. Tom began his attempt to take over magical Britain and rid the world of muggles. In his attempts, he also pursued immortality in the form of horcuxes. Horcruxes are physical objects that hold fragments of people's souls and can only be created through murder. In the end, he had seven horcruxes. To fully leave his muggle heritage behind, he changed his name to Voldemort (by twisting his very own name into the phrase 'I am Lord Voldemort'). However, as he began his rein as a dark lord, a prophecy fortelling his downfall came to be. The object of this prophecy turned out to be Harry Potter. Voldemort unintentionally fufilled part of the prophecy by murdering Harry's parents and accidentally creating a horcrux in the baby. Nearly every year of Harry's schooling, Voldemort faced and was defeated by the boy. In Harry's final year, he destroyed Voldemort completely.
