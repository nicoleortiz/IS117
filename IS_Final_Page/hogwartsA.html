Hogwarts: A History

Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry is the magical school catering to the British citizens of the world. It is located in the Highlands of Scotland under a spell that keeps away muggles (non-magical people). Children with magical ablities in Great Britain, Northern Ireland, and Ireland are automatically enrolled at birth, upon their eleventh birthday, the same children are sent an owl post to confirm their attendance (except in the case of muggle borns, then a Professor is sent).

With four founders: Godric Gryffindor, Helga Hufflepuff, Rowena Ravenclaw, and Salazar Slytherin, Hogwarts was created to protect and educate magical children. It was founded roughly around 990 A.D. and has continued to be one of the most prestigious magical learning schools in the world.
